Interacting with your Don

  1. Door Knocking: Between the hours of 12:00pm - 10:00pm feel free to knock on my door if you have questions, need help or just want to talk. If I'm there, I'll do my best to answer. If I'm not there, please reach out to me via email. After 10:00pm, please call the duty don.

Please do not follow my personal Instagram and expect me to follow you. I can't document what I don't know and that's why the less I know about your lives on social media, the better.
